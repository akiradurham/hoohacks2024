About The Developers

Welcome to our group's personal information page. We are a team of passionate individuals focused on creating interesting projects that we believe will succeed. From spending 4 hours fixing a bug that had us all scratching our heads to knocking out the main part of our app in that same amount of time brought us closer together as friends and as professionals. Experiencing the stress of a deadline, the constant pressure of competition, and the reward of presenting our final product, gave us insights into our careers as well as improving our real-world skills.

This project was built through Django, Python, Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS while utilizing Google's login and calendar API's to provide smooth Google suite integration!

We are all University of Virginia students with a major in Computer Science, exploring the 11th hackathon hosted by HooHacks. Creating an engaging web application and building full stack development skills are some of the key reasons we decided to participate in this year's hackathon, as well as the opportunity to meet so many other talented individuals
